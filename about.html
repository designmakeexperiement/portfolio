About Me

Hi! My name is Ji Su. I'm a digital product designer specializing in user experience and interaction design at Capital One. I used to be a lab researcher studying the neurobiological and molecular mechanisms of appetite control, but now I apply my problem solving skills I gained in the lab to designing and creating innovative products.

What I can do for your business

Design Strategy

I approach design problems with a clear high-level strategy and methodological approach. I can work with you to create detailed product and research plans to achieve your business outcomes and goals.


UX, Interaction, & UI Design

I design for the experiences of the users when they are using a product, with the goal of fulfilling both business objectives and user needs, with interfaces that are both functional and beautiful. Some regular methods I use are sketching, affinity mapping, wireframing, and prototyping.

Website Development

I can also translate my designs into code and build responsive landing/marketing pages or portfolio websites for your business.

My Process

My experience in lab research has equipped me with creative problem solving and project management skills needed to solve design challenges. I dissect all problems with a careful methodological approach, paying close attention to fine details as much as the big picture to ensure overall quality.
